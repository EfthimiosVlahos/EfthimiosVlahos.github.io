About Me

My journey thus far has taught me so many different things in life that I will be forever grateful for. Coming out of highschool, it isn't an exaggeration to say that I had no idea what I wanted to do with my life. The best decision I have made in my life to date is, without a doubt, the decision to pursue physics during my undergraduate: it truly opened my eyes to the world and brought me so much clarity in my life that I had no idea I needed. Loving physics as a passion taught me not only to improve my life in every aspect but also so many virtues that I try to live by to this day. After some serious thought about which career I wanted to pursue, I realized I needed to utilize all the knowledge physics has given me and try my best to change the world for the better, not to just theorize the origins of the universe which is kind of selfish if you really think about it: this is where programming came into my life. With programming, specifically data science or ML engineering, I would have the ability to change so many lives for the better and I realized that, in order to accomplish this task, I had to go for applied mathematics and pursue the discipline that best suits my ambitions which is to become a data scientist/ML engineer or, in general, a software engineer, at Stony Brook University.
